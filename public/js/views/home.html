“The question of what you want to own is actually the question of how you want to live your life.”
― Marie Kondō, The Life-Changing Magic of Tidying Up: The Japanese Art of Decluttering and Organizing



The notion that the more we have, the better our quality of life seems to be ingrained in our way of thinking. If you consider the time, energy, and money it takes to accumulate and maintain these things, you begin to see that having too much actually takes away from your quality of life. For over 10 years, with a 'living better with less' philosophy, I've helped my clients to move from consuming unconsciously to consciously; to living for and with what they truly love and need. It's not just about being organized; it's a new way of seeing your things. This approach is not only better for your space and for you spiritually; it's better for your wallet and even the planet.

De-clutter Service

The process of de-cluttering is all about making effective, efficient decisions about the items you want to keep and removing those, which are not enhancing your lifestyle. The Sort Club will work with you to evaluate these needs in a sensitive way so you and your property are left in the state you desire.

The De-Cluttering service concentrates on (but is not limited to):

  • Individual household rooms / Entire houses
  • Specific household areas
  • Cupboards
  • Attics/Lofts / Basements / Garages
  • Storage units

An initial consultation is recommended so the time on the de-cluttering day can be used most effectively. It is possible to have the consultation and de-cluttering service on the same day if required.

Wardrobes

Closets and wardrobes are one of the top areas people struggle with keeping organized. For this reason re-arranging and tidying up ones wardrobe can be a daunting task so many people will procrastinate with it. Let The Sort Club provide a professional consultation that will guide you to re-thinking your approach to your wardrobe and how you purchase clothes.

The Sort Club Wardrobe Service works like this:

  • Consultation and re-organization can be completed on the same day.
  • The Sort Club will help you to categorize your clothes leaving you with the wardrobe you always wanted.

Method for wardrobe clearing

  • Keep – Frequent use v’s Infrequent use, Not in season, Action – fix, mend, dry clean
  • Re-sell (we can organize this for you)
  • Charity – items in good condition but no longer being worn
  • Pass on to a friend
  • Archive
  • Memory
  • Rubbish – only genuine items that NO-ONE will be able to use
